Declaration of Independence

When in the Course of human events, it becomes necessary for one people to dissolve the political bands which have connected them with and to assume amoung the powers of the earth, the separate and equal station to which the Laws of Nature and of Nature's God entitled them, a decent respect to opinions of mankind requires that they should declare the causes which impel them to the seperation.
