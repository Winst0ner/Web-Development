Jumping on a trampoline is by far one of the best things to do in the summer. The more tricks you know, the more fun it is.
These 5 tricks are relativly easy to learn and look super cool once you have them down!
Once you have learned these skills, you will be well on your way to being a world class trampolinist. These tricks are the fundamentals used by even the athletes at the Freestyle World Championshps!
Sebbe Wennmalm is the best in the world and he used many variations of these tricks to win 1st place.

Trampoline Terms

Rotation
A rotation is a full spin in the forwards or backwards direction.
Twisting
Twisting is spinning in a 180 or 360 during a flip.
Tucking
Tucking is pulling your legs into yourself to rotate faster.
