Dub Back


Description

A dub back is a flip with two backwards rotations over your head.


Timing

Timing a cody.


Rotation

Backflip to stomach stuff.


Trampoline Terms

Kaboom
A three quarters backflip from your back to your feet.
Back Full
A backflip with a 360 degree turn.
Cody
A one and a quarter backflip from your stomach.
