Back Full


Description

A back full is a backflip with a 360 degree turn.


Twisting

You should start twisting as soon as you leave the trampoline. Lead with your arms and tuck your chin to the shoulder you are twisting over. Lead with your dominant hand(if you are right handed, you should twist to your left). Your legs should still be facing forwards until your upper body has twisted 45 degrees.


Landing

Landing back fulls can be sketchy becuase it is really hard to tell where you are in the flip when you are first learning them. The combination of rotation and twisting makes it very hard to see. Once you have gone completly inverted you should be able to spot the ground if you look up(down technically). Once you have spotted the ground, speed up or slow down your rotation to land on your feet.


Trampoline Terms

Kaboom
A three quarters backflip from your back to your feet.
Back Full
A backflip with a 360 degree turn.
Cody
A one and a quarter backflip from your stomach.
