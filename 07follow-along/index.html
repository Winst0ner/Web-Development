Our Story

Back in 10,000 BC someone in Jordan's northestern desert mixed flower, water and yeast.
He then dumped the resulting goop into the flames of his fire. Bread rose with the dawn of the Neolithic age.

That someone was Angelo. Ever since then, bread has continued to rise. And Angelo has continued to bake it.
He made a birthday cake for Ramses the Great in Egypt. He was there in Macedonia with celebratory cupcakes when Alexander the Great tamed the wild horse Bucephalus.

Angelo's bread is so good that he has stayed alive for 12,000 years just to share his discovery with the world.
Every morning he wakes from his slumber with the sole purpose of letting one more person experiance true joy.

You should defenetly buy his bread because it's that good.
